Why study with us?

Danger!

Yeah, you could go to a normal school and feel safe....Or
you could come here where we will actively throw you in to life threatening situations!

We will ease you in gently, where you will enjoy the delights of back firing spells exploding in your face. From there we will progress you to an assortment of dangerous creatures. You may even get the oppotunity to risk your own life against huge dragons for sport! cool as, right?

Even some of our staff are happy to concuss you with a book if you don't like studying.

Nature!

Do you like nature? Great! As you walk around our grounds
you will quickly learn that nature doesn't like you!

Get up close and personal with our willow tree and discover
that you never get a day off from life threatening situations
around here.

Do you like spiders? Great! we have GIANT hairy ones that
you can totally chat chat to before experiencing the thrill
of running for your life.

The Chosen One Package!!!

If you need more, don't worry- we got you!

Upgrade to our chosen one package and we will provide you with yor very own arch enemy who will stop at nothing to eviscerate you!

You'll also have an amazing time learning politics as your enemy takes control of the government and turns the whole world against you!

Still not enough?! Well, if you hang on in there you will finally get a boss battle where the stakes are legitimately high. how cool is that?!
